4 BR Penthouse

The Hotel Quadri overlooking the shores of Lake Garda promises its guests an unforgettable holiday in Sirmione, a resort renowned for its many tourist attractions, beautiful landscapes and spas. This graceful and recently renovated structure welcomes guests to a completely tranquil atmosphere, surrounded by nature where they can relax by the swimming pool or take a stroll to the nearby town centre and castle.

The Hotel Quadri is an excellent location to discover the artistic beauty of Sirmione, take advantage of the various sporting activities on the lake and enjoy all the recreational opportunities the territory offers from spas to trekking paths.
