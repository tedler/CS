Soccer is a game played by two teams on a rectangular field, with the object of driving the ball into the opponent's goal. The ball is controlled and advanced primarily by using the feet; only goalkeepers are allowed to handle the ball.

The duration of the session is to be 60 minutes in lenght with short drink breaks every 10 minutes. The session is to be highly dynamic and motivating for all players.
