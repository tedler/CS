I'm a freelance UX/UI Designer and Front End Developer living in Suffolk Park, Australia.

I specialize in creating simple and elegant user interfaces for web and mobile products. I also have a strong passion for brand identity, user experience research and coding.

I always had interest in finding simple digital solutions for complex problems. I enjoy creating pixel perfect designs, and my skills and passion go deeper than that:

my skills

  • Interaction Design
  • iOS and Android Design
  • Front-end Development

my passion

My ambition is to create beautiful products that people are happy to use on a daily basis, and at the same time keep it simple, find better answers, never stop learning and challenge myself.

I've been very lucky to work with great people on hundreds successful projects for a number of Australian brands such as FOXTEL, Office Works, Snooze, Sydney Airport etc.

My experience includes working for GeckoWebs and SwitchIT studios (both in Sydney). Now I'm a freelance designer with over 15 years experience in the digital industry.

Besides designing, you can find me with a strong coffee, paddling on a board or doing 10km runs.
