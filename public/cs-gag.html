GAG

Online video tutorials and training platform for soccer players

branding UX design UI design front-end

brief

A major part of the brief was to design the information architecture and user-flow to deliver user friendly interfaces for several hundreds soccer video lessons. This product should help young soccer players to develop their skills, plan soccer coaching sessions, obtain video drills and other link resources.

branding

Being an early-stage startup, the branding for the GAG company hadnâ€™t yet been created. My aim was to design an attractive and colourful brand concept with bright, vibrant colours to keep visual experience fun and friendly for kids.

wireframes

High fidility wireframes were produced to design a complex online training platform, which provides over 300 video lessons for 10 age groups, including session planner and user clipboard functionality.

ui style guide

From day one I worked closely with Global Ambition Group to make sure that we had simple, bright and near perfect style guide for user interfaces before proceeding to layout design stage.

layout design

Once the client was happy with branding and wireframes, I moved into Photoshop to put all required layout designs together to make functional ideas shine on the visual design level.

front-end development

On my final stage of this project I created required html\css templates and provided image assets for the development team to get their magic done and present the new product for the soccer community.
